Darwin's Delay by Stephen Jay Gould

Aside from the wonderful historical reflections, Gould’s mention of Arthur Koestler stands out. Koestler’s campaign against Darwinism-materialism was new to me (I don’t know too much detail about him clearly). What was on my radar though, was Koestler’s role as a “Cultural Cold Warrior” – one doesn’t need to do a world of research to get an idea of his role in the Congress of Cultural Freedom, the early CIA and anticommunist initiatives of the 20th Century.
