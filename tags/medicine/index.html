Cancer Prevention and Screening by Jason Fung; The Cancer Code

This is part 2 of the post on Jason Fung’s ‘Cancer Code’. I found this a remarkable case point in the iatrogenic potential of public health interventions in which the effect on mortality is masked by a surrogate of survival; early detection. When tracked for long enough time, early detection does more harm than good, and thus, fails as a successful surrogate. You can find part one here. I encourage everyone to read the entirety of Jason’s “The Cancer Code”.
